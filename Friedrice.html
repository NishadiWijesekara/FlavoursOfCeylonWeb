Sri Lankan Fried Rice Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice, preferably leftover and chilled
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, bell peppers)
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and chopped onion. Stir-fry until the onion becomes translucent.
  3. Add mixed vegetables and cook until they are tender-crisp.
  4. Push the vegetables to one side of the skillet and pour beaten eggs onto the empty side.
  5. Scramble the eggs until they are cooked through, then mix with the vegetables.
  6. Add cooked rice to the skillet. Stir-fry for a few minutes, breaking up any clumps.
  7. Add soy sauce and oyster sauce. Stir well to combine.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Continue to cook, stirring constantly, until the rice is heated through.
  10. Serve hot as a main dish or side dish.

Enjoy your Sri Lankan Fried Rice!
