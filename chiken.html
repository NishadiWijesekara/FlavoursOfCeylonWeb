Sri Lankan Chicken Curry Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1/2 cup thick coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2 cardamom pods
  • 2 cloves
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onions, minced garlic, grated ginger, and slit green chilies. Saute until onions turn golden brown.
  3. Add chicken pieces to the pan. Stir well to coat with onions and spices.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes, curry powder, chili powder, turmeric powder, cinnamon stick, cardamom pods, and cloves. Mix well.
  5. Cover the pan and cook on low heat until the chicken is cooked through.
  6. Once the chicken is cooked, pour thick coconut milk into the pan. Stir and simmer for a few more minutes.
  7. Adjust seasoning according to taste.
  8. Serve hot with rice or roti.

Enjoy your Sri Lankan Chicken Curry!
