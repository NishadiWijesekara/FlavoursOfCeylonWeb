Sri Lankan King Coconut Water Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 fresh king coconut

Instructions

  1. Using a sharp knife, carefully open the top of the king coconut.
  2. Pour the refreshing coconut water into a glass or serve it directly from the coconut.
  3. Enjoy the natural goodness of Sri Lankan King Coconut Water!

Enjoy your Sri Lankan Coconut Water!
