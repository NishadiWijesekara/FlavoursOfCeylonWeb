Sri Lankan Parippu Curry Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup red lentils (masoor dal)
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 2 green chilies, sliced
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 cup thick coconut milk
  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Rinse the red lentils thoroughly under running water.
  2. In a pot, combine red lentils and water. Bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer until lentils are tender (about 15-20 minutes).
  4. Heat coconut oil in a pan over medium heat.
  5. Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  6. Add chopped onions, minced garlic, grated ginger, and sliced green chilies. Saute until onions are soft.
  7. Add turmeric powder, chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
  8. Add cooked lentils to the pan and mix well with the spice mixture.
  9. Pour thick coconut milk into the pan. Stir and bring to a simmer.
  10. Cover and cook on low heat for a few more minutes.
  11. Adjust seasoning according to taste.
  12. Serve hot with rice or roti.

Enjoy your Sri Lankan Parippu Curry!
