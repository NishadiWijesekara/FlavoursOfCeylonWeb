Sri Lankan Dodol Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice flour
  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 2 cups jaggery (palm sugar), grated
  • 1/2 cup treacle (kithul pani)
  • 1/4 cup cashew nuts, chopped
  • 1/4 cup pumpkin seeds
  • 1/4 cup raisins
  • 1/4 cup grated coconut
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. In a large pan, combine rice flour and coconut milk. Stir well to form a smooth paste.
  2. Add grated jaggery and treacle to the pan. Mix well until the jaggery melts and the mixture thickens.
  3. Add chopped cashew nuts, pumpkin seeds, raisins, grated coconut, cardamom powder, nutmeg powder, and salt. Mix well.
  4. Cook the mixture over low heat, stirring continuously, until it becomes thick and starts to leave the sides of the pan.
  5. Grease a flat tray or dish with coconut oil.
  6. Pour the thickened dodol mixture into the greased tray and spread it evenly using a spatula.
  7. Allow the dodol to cool and set for several hours or overnight.
  8. Once set, cut the dodol into small pieces or diamond shapes.
  9. Serve and enjoy your Sri Lankan Dodol!
