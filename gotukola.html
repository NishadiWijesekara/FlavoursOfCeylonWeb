Sri Lankan Gotu Kola Salad Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups fresh gotu kola leaves (also known as pennywort), finely chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup fresh coconut, grated
  • 1/4 cup lime juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine chopped gotu kola leaves, chopped onions, chopped green chilies, and grated fresh coconut.
  2. In a small pan, heat vegetable oil over medium heat.
  3. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  4. Pour the hot oil and mustard seeds over the salad mixture.
  5. Add lime juice and salt to taste.
  6. Toss everything together until well combined.
  7. Adjust seasoning according to taste.
  8. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving to allow the flavors to meld.
  9. Serve chilled as a refreshing salad.

Enjoy your Sri Lankan Gotu Kola Salad!
