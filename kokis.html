Sri Lankan Kokis Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice flour
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine rice flour, coconut milk, egg, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well to form a smooth batter.
  2. Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
  3. Dip the kokis mold into the hot oil for a few seconds to heat it up.
  4. Dip the heated mold into the batter, making sure it coats the mold evenly.
  5. Place the coated mold back into the hot oil and fry until the kokis turns golden brown and crispy.
  6. Remove the fried kokis from the oil and drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  7. Repeat the process with the remaining batter.
  8. Let the kokis cool completely before serving.

Enjoy your Sri Lankan Kokis!
