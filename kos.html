Sri Lankan Kos Curry Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cabbage, shredded
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 cup thick coconut milk
  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat coconut oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add mustard seeds and fenugreek seeds. Let them splutter.
  3. Add sliced onions and saut√© until they turn translucent.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft.
  5. Add shredded cabbage, turmeric powder, chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
  6. Pour thick coconut milk into the pan and mix well.
  7. Cover and cook on low heat until the cabbage is tender.
  8. Adjust seasoning according to taste.
  9. Serve hot with rice or roti.

Enjoy your Sri Lankan Kos Curry!
