Sri Lankan Lavariya Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice flour
  • 1/2 cup grated coconut
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup palm sugar (jaggery), grated
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Banana leaves or parchment paper, for wrapping

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine rice flour, grated coconut, coconut milk, grated palm sugar, and salt. Mix well to form a smooth dough.
  2. Divide the dough into small portions and shape each portion into an oval or cylindrical shape.
  3. Cut banana leaves into rectangular pieces large enough to wrap each dough portion.
  4. Place a dough portion on each piece of banana leaf and fold the leaf around it to enclose the dough completely.
  5. Secure the wrapped dough by tying it with kitchen twine or by folding the edges of the banana leaf.
  6. Steam the wrapped lavariya over medium heat for about 20-25 minutes or until cooked through.
  7. Remove from heat and let it cool slightly before serving.
  8. Unwrap the lavariya and serve warm or at room temperature.

Enjoy your Sri Lankan Lavariya!
