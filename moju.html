Sri Lankan Wambatu Moju Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggplants (brinjals), thinly sliced
  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 green chilies, sliced
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add thinly sliced eggplants to the pan. Fry until they are golden brown. Remove from the pan and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  4. Add thinly sliced onions and green chilies. Saute until onions turn translucent.
  5. Add fried eggplants back to the pan.
  6. Add vinegar, sugar, chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well.
  7. Cook for a few more minutes until the flavors are well combined and the eggplants are tender.
  8. Remove from heat and let it cool.
  9. Transfer to a serving dish and serve as a side dish with rice or bread.

Enjoy your Sri Lankan Wambatu Moju!
