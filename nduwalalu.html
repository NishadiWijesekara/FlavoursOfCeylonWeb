Sri Lankan Undu Walalu Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup black gram (undu), soaked overnight
  • 1 cup jaggery (palm sugar), grated
  • 1/4 cup coconut milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions

  1. Drain the soaked black gram and grind it into a smooth paste using a blender or food processor. Add a little water if necessary.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the ground black gram paste, grated jaggery, coconut milk, cardamom powder, and salt. Mix well to form a smooth batter.
  3. Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
  4. Drop spoonfuls of the batter into the hot oil and fry until they turn golden brown and crispy.
  5. Remove the fried undu walalu from the oil and drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  6. Allow the undu walalu to cool slightly before serving.
  7. Serve and enjoy your Sri Lankan Undu Walalu!
