Sri Lankan Papaya Smoothie Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe papaya, peeled and seeded
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1/2 teaspoon lime juice
  • Ice cubes, as desired

Instructions

  1. Cut the ripe papaya into chunks.
  2. In a blender, combine papaya chunks, milk, honey, lime juice, and ice cubes.
  3. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  4. Pour into glasses and serve chilled.
  5. Enjoy the tropical goodness of Sri Lankan Papaya Smoothie!
