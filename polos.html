Sri Lankan Polos Curry Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups young green jackfruit (polos), chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 2 green chilies, sliced
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • 1 cup thick coconut milk
  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat coconut oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onions, minced garlic, grated ginger, and sliced green chilies. Saute until onions are soft.
  3. Add chopped green jackfruit (polos) to the pan. Stir well.
  4. Add turmeric powder, chili powder, curry powder, and salt. Mix until the jackfruit is coated with spices.
  5. Pour thick coconut milk into the pan. Stir and bring to a simmer.
  6. Cover the pan and cook on low heat until the jackfruit is tender and the curry has thickened.
  7. Adjust seasoning according to taste.
  8. Serve hot with rice or roti.

Enjoy your Sri Lankan Polos Curry!
