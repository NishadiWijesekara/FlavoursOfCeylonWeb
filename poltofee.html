Sri Lankan Coconut Toffee (Pol Toffee) Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups grated coconut
  • 1 cup jaggery (palm sugar), grated
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. In a saucepan, combine grated jaggery and water. Heat over low heat until the jaggery melts completely.
  2. Add grated coconut, cardamom powder, and salt to the saucepan. Mix well.
  3. Cook the mixture over low heat, stirring continuously, until it thickens and starts to leave the sides of the pan.
  4. Grease a flat tray or dish with coconut oil.
  5. Pour the thickened coconut toffee mixture into the greased tray and spread it evenly using a spatula.
  6. Allow the coconut toffee to cool and set for several hours or overnight.
  7. Once set, cut the coconut toffee into small squares or rectangles.
  8. Serve and enjoy your Sri Lankan Coconut Toffee (Pol Toffee)!
