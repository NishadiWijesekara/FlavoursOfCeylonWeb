Sri Lankan Potato Curry Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 medium potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 green chilies, sliced
  • 1/2 cup thick coconut milk
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add chopped onions and sliced green chilies. Saute until onions turn golden brown.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and curry leaves. Cook until tomatoes are soft.
  5. Add curry powder, chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well.
  6. Add diced potatoes to the pan. Stir well to coat with the spice mixture.
  7. Pour thick coconut milk into the pan. Stir gently to combine.
  8. Cover the pan and simmer on low heat until the potatoes are cooked through and the curry has thickened.
  9. Adjust seasoning according to taste.
  10. Serve hot with rice or roti.

Enjoy your Sri Lankan Potato Curry!
