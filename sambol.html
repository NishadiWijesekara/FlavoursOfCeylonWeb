Sri Lankan Sambol Curry Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups freshly grated coconut
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add freshly grated coconut to the pan and dry roast until lightly golden brown. Remove from heat and let it cool.
  3. In the same pan, heat some oil.
  4. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  5. Add chopped onions, minced garlic, chopped green chilies, and curry leaves. Saute until onions turn translucent.
  6. Add chopped tomatoes, chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Cook until tomatoes are soft.
  7. Add the roasted coconut to the pan and mix well with the spice mixture.
  8. Remove from heat and let it cool slightly.
  9. Once cooled, add lime juice and mix well.
  10. Adjust seasoning according to taste.
  11. Serve as a side dish with rice or roti.

Enjoy your Sri Lankan Sambol Curry!
