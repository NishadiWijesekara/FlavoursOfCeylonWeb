Sri Lankan Waw Malu Curry Recipe

Ingredients

  • 500g lake fish (waw malu), cleaned and cut into pieces
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 3 green chilies, slit
  • 1/2 cup thick coconut milk
  • 1 tablespoon tamarind paste
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onions and saut√© until they turn golden brown.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes, slit green chilies, and curry leaves. Cook until tomatoes are soft.
  4. Add chili powder, turmeric powder, fenugreek seeds, and salt. Mix well.
  5. Add tamarind paste and mix well with the spices.
  6. Place the fish pieces in the pan and coat them with the spice mixture.
  7. Pour thick coconut milk into the pan. Stir gently to combine.
  8. Cover the pan and simmer on low heat until the fish is cooked through and the curry has thickened.
  9. Adjust seasoning according to taste.
  10. Serve hot with rice or roti.

Enjoy your Sri Lankan Waw Malu Curry!
