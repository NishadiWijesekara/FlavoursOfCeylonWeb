Sri Lankan Woodapple Juice Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 woodapples
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon lime juice

Instructions

  1. Break open the woodapples and scoop out the pulp.
  2. In a blender, combine woodapple pulp, water, sugar, and lime juice.
  3. Blend until smooth.
  4. Strain the mixture to remove any solids.
  5. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour.
  6. Serve cold and enjoy Sri Lankan Woodapple Juice!
